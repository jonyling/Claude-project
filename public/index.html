About

From practitioner
to enabler.

I'm Jony Ling, based in Singapore — a mid-career professional who made a deliberate pivot into AI, machine learning, and data analytics. I arrived here through coursework, hands-on capstone projects, and Singapore's AI Apprenticeship Programme (AIAP Batch 22): one of the more rigorous government-run technical assessments in the region.

My goal isn't just to apply these tools myself. It's to help small and medium-sized businesses understand which AI investments are worth making — and how to implement them without needing an in-house data science team on the payroll.

I've worked on problems across financial markets, Singapore's job market, and global AI adoption trends. I can read the research, write the code, and explain the results to someone who has never heard of gradient descent.

"Most SMEs don't need a data scientist. They need someone who can show them which three decisions AI can actually help with."
— Jony Ling, Singapore
